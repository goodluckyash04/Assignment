Que 2.What is the difference between OOP and POP?
Ans:
-OOP is Object Oriented Programming while POP is Procedural Oriented Programming.
-In OOP, the major emphasis is on data rather than procedure (function) while In POP, a program is written as a sequence of procedures or function.
-OOP is Bottom-up approach while POP is Top-down approach.
-OOP is use for solving big problems while POP is used for small problems.
